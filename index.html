Cat Senses

Cats have excellent night vision and can see at one-sixth the light level required for human vision.

A cat's hearing is most sensitive between 500 Hz and 32 kHz. It can detect a wide frequency range, from 55 Hz to 79 kHz, surpassing human and canine hearing.

Cats have movable whiskers (vibrissae) that help navigate spaces and detect objects, even sensing air currents in darkness.
